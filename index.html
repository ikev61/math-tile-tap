Privacy Policy for Math Tile Tap

Effective Date: October 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Math Tile Tap application ("the App") handles the data you provide while using the service.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The Math Tile Tap App is designed to run entirely within your device's browser environment. No data is transmitted to, stored on, or shared with external servers, cloud services, or any third party by the developer.

A. Locally Stored Data

All user data is stored exclusively on your device within your browser's Local Storage. This data is only accessible by the App when you use it on the same device and browser. Clearing your browser's history or cookies may delete this data.

The data stored locally includes:

Data Type Purpose Details
Player Name Required to identify the user and manage game progress. Stored as plain text.
Game Progress To save and resume gameplay. Includes current level, best score, last score, streak, accuracy, and a history of past game sessions (date, score, level, accuracy).
Optional Profile Data To personalize the Student Profile. Includes School Name, School Address, and Date of Birth (if provided by the user).
Preferences To remember your settings. Includes Dark Mode preference and Audio On/Off setting.

2. Use of Collected Data

The data collected locally is used solely for the purpose of running the game and improving the user experience on your device:

  • Tracking Game Performance: To show you your current level, best score, and accuracy over time.
  • Saving Progress: To allow you to resume your game progress (level, score) when you revisit the App.
  • Personalization: To display your name and profile details in the Student Profile and print-out reports.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because no user data is collected or stored outside of your local browser environment, there is no data sharing, disclosure, or sale to any third parties by the developer.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics services or advertising networks that collect personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information externally, it complies with standards related to children's online privacy. All data, including optional profile fields, remains on the user's device.

6. Your Data Rights

Since your data is stored locally, you control it entirely:

  • Access and Correction: You can view and edit your profile data directly through the Student Profile screen within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your stored progress for a specific player using the "Reset Player Progress" option on the start screen, or by clearing your browser's local storage for this **application**.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the Effective Date of this Privacy Policy. Since the App is single-serving and does not send data externally, changes will primarily concern local data handling transparency.
